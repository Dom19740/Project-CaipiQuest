Privacy Policy for CaipiQuest

Last Updated:

Important Note: CaipiQuest is a family-friendly fruit-matching bingo game. While the name references tropical fruits commonly used in beverages, this app contains no alcohol-related content and is suitable for all ages.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information You Provide:

  • Email Address: When you create an account or sign in, we collect your email address for authentication purposes.
  • Display Name: You may choose a display name that will be visible to other players in multiplayer games.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Game Data: We collect information about your gameplay, including your bingo grid selections, game progress, and achievements.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected to ensure compatibility and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CaipiQuest game service
  • Enable multiplayer functionality and game room features
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts
  • Improve our game and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about game updates or support issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted backend service provider. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy:

  • Supabase: We use Supabase as our backend service provider to store and manage user data and game information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

5. Children's Privacy

CaipiQuest is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of communications from us
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
